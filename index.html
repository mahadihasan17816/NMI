About NMI

There are so many people who are unable to use their hands or has suffered a loss of limb and for that they can not use smartphone properly by their hand. To aid these disabled mobile users to use smartphone and utilize most of its functionalities, we developed a Nose-Mobile-Interaction (NMI) system for them to seamlessly operate smartphone. This system has a cursor pointer which can be moved by the userâ€™s nose and the user will be able to execute different smartphone operations like tap, drag, swipe, scroll, etc. by only showing the teeth.
